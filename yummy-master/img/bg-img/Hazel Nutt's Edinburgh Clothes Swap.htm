About

  1. Home
  2. About

I'd like to introduce ourselves! We are Stevie and Nia, otherwise known as Hazel Nutt. We are both masters students at Edinburgh Uni and wanted to create a platform which helps university students be more sustainable. The problem of fast fashion is detrimental to the planet, and a clothes swap is one simple way in which everyone can help reduce their ecological footprint.

Happy Swapping!
